# Monthly review

Query, then return to `../SKILL.md § Unified flow` step 2. Monthly surfaces
items but **the user decides** — no auto-action.

## Query

```bash
loopany artifact list --kind mission --status active
```

Plus recent tasks (30d) for alignment check.

## Mission alignment

→ `../../loopany-core/kinds/mission.md § Playbook § Alignment monitoring`

## Structural drift — domains

All three must hold before proposing a `[change]` task:

1. ≥ 5 artifacts with an unenabled domain
2. ≥ 2 weeks of accumulation
3. Distinguishable from existing domains

## Structural drift — kinds

Both must hold before proposing a `[change]` task:

1. ≥ 3 notes sharing a body skeleton, ≥ 2 weeks
2. Passes the 4-question test (`../../loopany-core/conventions/taxonomy.md`)

Forward-only — no migration of existing artifacts.
